Science Department (STEM)

Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Computer Studies.

Facilities: Physics and Chemistry labs, Biology lab, computer lab with internet access and project work spaces.

Humanities Department

Subjects: English, Kiswahili, History & Government, Geography, Religious Education, Social Studies.

Emphasis on critical thinking, research, civic education and communication skills.

Arts & Sports Department

Subjects: Music, Drama, Visual Arts, Design & Technology and Physical Education.

We nurture creative talent and provide structured athletics and sports programs.

Guidance & Counselling

Student welfare services including career guidance, counselling support and life skills training.

Movements & Spiritual Groups

Active student movements including Christian Union, Catholic groups and Islamic Society. These provide pastoral care, mentorship and forums for moral development and spiritual life on campus.

Boarding Department

Responsible for student accommodation, dormitory management, dining & kitchen services and the general welfare of boarders. The department ensures safe housing, nutritious meals and pastoral supervision through house parents and senior prefects.
