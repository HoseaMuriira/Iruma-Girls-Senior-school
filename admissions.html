Admission Overview

IRUMA GIRLS SENIOR SCHOOL admits learners into Junior and Senior secondary levels following the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBE). We welcome applicants who demonstrate readiness for the school's academic and co‑curricular programmes.

Entry Requirements

  • Completed application form (online or printed)
  • Copy of KCPE (for learners joining junior secondary) or latest school report
  • Birth certificate or national ID
  • Medical declaration and up-to-date immunization record

How to Apply (Online)

  1. Complete the online application form below or download the PDF application.
  2. Attach scanned copies of the required documents.
  3. Submit the form and pay the application processing fee (if any).
  4. Shortlisted applicants will be contacted for interview/assessment.

Fee Structure (Indicative)

Fees vary by level and optional extras. The table below shows typical items (local currency KES).

  • Tuition (per term): 15,000 – 25,000
  • Boarding (if applicable): 8,000 – 12,000
  • Examination & administration: 2,000
  • Uniform & textbooks (one time): 6,000 – 10,000
