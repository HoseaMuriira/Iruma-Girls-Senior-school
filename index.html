Welcome to IRUMA GIRLS SENIOR SCHOOL

We are a girls' secondary school in Tharaka Nithi County following Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBE). We offer triple pathways — STEM, Humanities, Arts & Sports — to nurture holistic skills, leadership and lifelong learning.

Chief Principal's Message

Welcome to Iruma Girls Senior School. Our commitment is to deliver a balanced education that nurtures academic excellence, character and practical skills. We champion the Competency-Based Curriculum and encourage every student to discover and develop their unique talents.

Mrs.Mary Mugo
Principal

Deputy Principal (Academic)

Our academic program focuses on active, inquiry-based learning and real-life competencies. We support learners with guidance, enrichment and remedial help so every pupil can thrive.

Mr. P. M. Mwangi
Deputy Principal

Mission & Vision

To nurture competent, confident and compassionate young women through a skills-based curriculum and a supportive learning environment.
